Services


Guidance and Counseling: OFFICE OF GUIDANCE AND COUNSELING

The Loyola Schools Office of Guidance & Counseling (LSOGC) offers programs and services designed to equip the
students with the necessary tools for self-actualization in the context of personal, familial and societal
realities.

Work or Internship Opportunities: ATENEO PLACEMENT OFFICE

The Ateneo Placement Office (APO) assists graduates in finding employment appropriate to their individuality
for positive contribution to self, family, and society through a comprehensive Career Development Program
and Networking

Religious Services: OFFICE OF THE CAMPUS MINISTRY

The Loyola Schools Office of the Campus Ministry, as a component of a Catholic and Jesuit institution, partakes
of the Church mission of forming a community of disciples through renewed integral evangelization by striving to
awaken the Loyola Schools community to the love of God

Immersion Activites: OFFICE FOR SOCIAL CONCERN AND INVOLVEMENT

The Office for Social Concern and Involvement (OSCI) is the conscientization arm of the Ateneo de Manila
University that aims to increase social awareness and involvement by being more responsive to the struggle of
the marginalized sectors of our society

Non-Academic Student Support Services:


OFFICE OF THE ASSOCIATE DEAN FOR STUDENT AFFAIRS

The Office of the Associate Dean for Student Affairs (ADSA) attends to the non-academic life of
students at the Ateneo. In general, this means providing the relevant support services

Health Services: INFIRMARY

The college infirmary offers free consultation, physical exams, emergency treatment and starter doses for
students or staff needing medical attention. It has three doctors, on-call, a regular administrative staff, and
four beds (2 for females, 2 for males). The infirmary is located at the ground floor of the Social Sciences
buildings
